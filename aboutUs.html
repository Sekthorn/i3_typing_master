About Typing Master

Welcome to Typing Master, a project developed to help users improve their typing skills in a fun and interactive way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make typing practice accessible, engaging, and effective for everyone. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn proper typing techniques or an advanced user wanting to increase your speed, Typing Master is designed for you.

The Team

This project was developed as part of the Introduction to Programming Environments course at ITC. Our team is dedicated to creating tools that enhance learning experiences through technology.
